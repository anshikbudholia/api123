WELCOME TO The Music Cafe


Our radio club group went to lunch at Music Cafe recently. We started with 4 people attending, but ended up with 8. The staff were so accommodating, seating the additional people as near as possible to us. The server was friendly and helpful. I got my favorite - tuna fish salad on croissant and raspberry iced tea. I picked coleslaw as my side dish. Others got pizza - it looked and smelled terrific and they said it was very good. Another got the cream of crab soup and loved it. Someone else got a huge salad, and another got the "The Sinatra" - ham, turkey, bacon, gouda on pretzel bread. They said the sandwich was tasty though not outstanding. I've been to the Music Cafe numerous times and always enjoyed the food. Service is always great. On Saturdays, they often have musicians playing guitars and singing. It's a friendly, no frills kind of place with quick service and tasty food..



@ MUSIC_Cafe

In the heart of Damascus, Music Cafe shares a site with a vet. Indoors is clean and friendly with music related decor. Outside patio has plants and seems more like the beach than the suburbs. Outstanding coffee from a regional roaster. Live local music. A ton of sandwiches and other casual dishes. Vegan/veg options. Friendly staff. Clean bathrooms. COVID conscious. Will go again and again.
